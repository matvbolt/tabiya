# Tabiya ◆

A chess platform where you play, learn and earn on one board.

**Live:** <a href="https://tabiya-one.vercel.app" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">tabiya-one.vercel.app</a>

Tabiya blends online chess with an opening-theory engine and a small creator
economy. Play rated games and friendly challenges in real time, train against a
Stockfish bot with on-board hints, study famous openings, follow the world
rankings, read and write community guides, and spend the coins you earn on
profile customization and partner perks.

## What you can do

- **Play online** — rated games and training, matchmaking by rating, friend
  challenges, live clocks, and move-by-move feedback.
- **Play the bot** — Stockfish at several strengths and time controls, with
  hints from opening theory or the engine, and chess.com-style move ratings.
- **Learn** — opening principles and every system explained with diagrams;
  read community articles, mark them helpful, or write your own.
- **Rankings** — the world's top players and real upcoming championships.
- **Earn & customize** — collect ChessCoin from games and articles, then spend
  it on nickname badges, nickname colors and partner discounts.
- **Profile & friends** — avatar, Elo rating and history, friends and public
  profiles. Dark/light theme, English/Russian.

Built with React, TypeScript and Vite, a Stockfish 18 WASM engine, and Supabase.
